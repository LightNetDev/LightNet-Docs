Now that your site is up and running, let's explore how to use the developer documentation to extend it.

Our documentation is structured to guide you through the complete process of creating and maintaining your site:

- **Build** - Learn how to develop your site locally
- **Add Content** - Manage and organize your site's content and media
- **Ship** - Deploy your site to the internet
- **Run** - Maintain and update your live site
- **Reference** - Browse comprehensive API documentation
- **Advance Your Journey** - Learn how to contribute and explore LightNet internals
